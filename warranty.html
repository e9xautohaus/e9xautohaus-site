Warranty & Quality

1. Our Philosophy

Every part produced by E9X AutoHaus is designed, inspected, and packaged with a single objective: to deliver hardware we would confidently install on our own cars. No shortcuts. No compromises. No inflated marketing claims.

2. Manufacturing Standards

All products are manufactured using controlled processes, dimensional checks, and batch traceability where practical. Small-batch production allows for stricter quality oversight compared to mass-market alternatives.

3. Limited Warranty Coverage

E9X AutoHaus products are covered by a limited manufacturing defect warranty from the date of purchase. This warranty covers defects in material or workmanship under normal intended use.

4. What This Warranty Does Not Cover

  • Incorrect installation or torque procedures
  • Track abuse beyond reasonable use
  • Damage from impact, collision, or misuse
  • Wear items subjected to heat cycling and motorsport conditions

5. Motorsport Use Disclaimer

Our products are designed to tolerate aggressive street and track conditions. However, motorsport inherently carries increased mechanical risk. By purchasing and installing E9X AutoHaus components, you acknowledge and accept this risk.

6. Claims Process

If you believe a product has a manufacturing defect, contact info@e9xautohaus.com with your order details and clear photography of the issue. Each case is assessed individually and fairly.

7. No Marketing Warranty

We do not sell fantasy. Claims are based on engineering, materials, and application — not hype, not influencer noise.
