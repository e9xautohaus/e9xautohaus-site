Built by Enthusiasts

E9X AutoHaus exists because we’re E9X owners ourselves. Every product starts with a simple question: “Would we trust this on our own car, on track, at speed?” If the answer isn’t a clear yes, it doesn’t leave development.

Born from the Platform

This isn’t a generic parts store. It’s a small, Brisbane-based operation focused on hardware for BMW E9X and E8X chassis — cars we daily, build, break and improve. The first products we’re releasing are the exact parts we wished existed when we started modifying our own cars.

Designed for the Drive

Everything we do is built around how these cars are actually used:

  • Street-driven cars that still see track days
  • OEM+ builds that care about reliability and feel
  • Owners who value quality hardware over hype

We’re not trying to reinvent every part on the car. We focus on the hardware that matters — the pieces that make your car safer, sharper and easier to live with on and off the track.

Trusted at the Limit

Our goal isn’t to slap a logo on catalogue parts. Wherever possible, we work with suppliers who understand performance applications, and we build around real-world use:

  • Repeated heat cycles
  • Track kerbs and braking zones
  • Frequent wheel changes and maintenance

If a part doesn’t behave predictably at the limit, it doesn’t belong on the car.

Zero Compromise

“Zero Compromise” for us doesn’t mean overpriced or over-marketed. It means:

  • We refuse to cut corners on materials or design just to hit a cheaper cost
  • We’d rather release fewer, better products than a huge catalogue of average ones
  • We price parts to be fair, not to chase artificial “luxury” margins

Built Small on Purpose

E9X AutoHaus is deliberately small-batch. That means:

  • We can respond to feedback quickly
  • We can improve designs over time instead of locking them in
  • We stay close to the community actually using the parts

Every early customer helps shape what this brand becomes — from the first stud kits to future hardware and track-focused essentials.

From One Owner to Another

If you’ve found your way here, you probably care about the same things we do: clean builds, honest parts, and cars that feel right when you push them.
