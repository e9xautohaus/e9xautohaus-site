Why E9X AutoHaus Exists

E9X AutoHaus was created for one simple reason: we’re E9X owners ourselves. Every product we make exists because we wanted something better for our own cars — hardware that was reliable, affordable, track-ready and completely free of unnecessary hype.

E9X AutoHaus started the same way most good ideas do – in a shed, under a car, wondering why the parts we actually needed for street + track use were either overpriced, overcomplicated or just not built the way we’d trust.

Born in Brisbane. Tested in the Real World.

We don’t operate out of a corporate office. We build, design and test our parts in the same garages, driveways and local tracks as the people who buy from us. This is a brand built around:

  • Real cars
  • Real owners
  • Real street and track use

When something doesn’t fit, isn’t good enough, or could be improved, we fix it. No shortcuts. No inflated margins. Just hardware we’d trust on our own builds — because we do.

Designed for the Drive

Every product we release starts with a clear principle: “Would we run this on our own E9X?” If the answer isn’t “absolutely,” it doesn’t leave development.

Trusted at the Limit

Our parts are created with both the street and the track in mind. We aim for the sweet spot between:

  • OEM+ reliability
  • Motorsport practicality
  • Affordable, accessible performance

Whether you’re daily driving, doing mountain runs, or chasing PBs at QR or Lakeside, our hardware is built to handle it.

Made for the Community

E9X AutoHaus isn’t trying to be the next massive aftermarket company. We’re here to make high-value, properly engineered hardware for a platform we genuinely love. Every purchase supports the development of new products, better tooling, and future expansions into the E8X and broader BMW enthusiast world.

From the first stud conversion kits through to future “track essentials”, everything follows one rule: if we wouldn’t run it on our own cars, it doesn’t ship.
