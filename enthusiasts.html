Built By Enthusiasts

E9X AutoHaus was created for one simple reason: we’re E9X owners ourselves. Every product we make exists because we wanted something better for our own cars — hardware that was reliable, affordable, track-ready and completely free of unnecessary hype.

Born in Brisbane. Tested in the Real World.

We don’t operate out of a corporate office. We build, design and test our parts in the same garages, driveways and local tracks as the people who buy from us. This is a brand built around:

  • Real cars
  • Real owners
  • Real street and track use

When something doesn’t fit, isn’t good enough, or could be improved, we fix it. No shortcuts. No corners cut. No inflated margins. Just hardware we’d trust on our own builds — because we do.

Designed for the Drive

Every product we release starts with a clear principle: “Would we run this on our own E9X?”

If the answer isn’t “absolutely,” it doesn’t leave development.

Trusted at the Limit

Our parts are created with both the street and the track in mind. We aim for the sweet spot between:

  • OEM+ reliability
  • Motorsport practicality
  • Affordable, accessible performance

Whether you’re daily driving, doing mountain runs, or chasing PBs at QR or Lakeside, our hardware is built to handle it.

Zero Compromise

Other brands inflate their margins. We don’t.

Zero Compromise doesn’t mean overpriced. It means:

  • We don’t cut corners
  • We don’t chase trends
  • We don’t add nonsense or gimmicks
  • We don’t upsell parts you don’t need

We build only what we’d personally run — and what the E9X community actually needs.

Made for the Community

E9X AutoHaus isn’t trying to be the next massive aftermarket company. We’re here to make high-value, properly engineered hardware for a platform we genuinely love.

Every purchase supports the development of new products, better tooling, and future expansions into the E8X and broader BMW enthusiast world.

Thanks for supporting a small, passionate, Brisbane-based brand. Whether you’re here for your first stud conversion kit or waiting for future products — we appreciate you.
