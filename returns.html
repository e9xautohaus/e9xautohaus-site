Returns & Refunds

Applies to products sold under the E9X AutoHaus name.

E9X AutoHaus aims to provide high-quality, enthusiast-grade parts. Because many of our products are small-batch and purpose-built, returns are handled fairly and on a case-by-case basis.

Change of Mind

We are not required to provide a refund for change of mind. However, depending on the condition of the item and timing, we may offer:

  • Store credit; or
  • An exchange, where reasonable.

Any change-of-mind consideration requires the item to be unused, in original condition and packaging, and you will be responsible for return shipping.

Faulty or Incorrect Items

If you believe a product is defective or has been incorrectly supplied:

  • Contact us within 7 days of receiving your order.
  • Provide proof of purchase and clear photos or details of the issue.

We will assess the situation and determine whether repair, replacement or refund is appropriate, in line with Australian Consumer Law (where applicable).

Installation Responsibility

Incorrect installation, misuse, or use outside the product’s intended purpose is not considered a product defect.

It is your responsibility to ensure parts are installed by a competent person and checked regularly, especially when used on track or under high load.

Returns Process

Do not return any items without contacting us first. Unauthorised returns may not be accepted.
