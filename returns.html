Returns & Refunds

E9X AutoHaus aims to provide high quality products for E9X and E8X BMWs. Because many items are small-batch and enthusiast-focused, returns are handled fairly and on a case-by-case basis.

Change of Mind

We are not required to provide a refund for change of mind. In some cases we may offer store credit or exchange at our discretion, provided the product is unused, in original condition and packaging.

Faulty or Incorrect Items

If you believe a product is defective or has been incorrectly supplied:

  • Contact us as soon as possible after receiving your order.
  • Provide proof of purchase and clear photos or details of the issue.
  • Do not continue to use a part you believe may be unsafe.

We will assess whether repair, replacement or refund is appropriate in line with Australian Consumer Law (if applicable).

Installation & Misuse

Returns or refunds will not be offered where issues arise due to incorrect installation, misuse, lack of maintenance, or use outside the intended purpose of the product.

Shipping Costs

Where a return is accepted, you may be responsible for shipping the item back to us unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Contact

For any questions about returns or to lodge an issue, contact info@e9xautohaus.com.
