Returns & Refunds

E9X AutoHaus aims to provide high quality products. Because many of our parts are small-batch and enthusiast-focused, returns are managed fairly and on a case-by-case basis.

Change of Mind

We are not required to provide a refund for change of mind. However, we may offer store credit or exchange at our discretion.

Faulty or Incorrect Items

If a product is defective or incorrectly supplied:

  • Contact us within 7 days of receiving your order
  • Provide proof of purchase and photos if requested
  • We will assess whether replacement, repair or refund is appropriate

Installation Responsibility

Incorrect installation or misuse is not considered a product defect.
